Art Classes

We offer classes for every type of artist! Whether you are an experienced painter or just starting out, a class at Art Haven is just what you need to improve your skills. We offer classes every week, but you are free to come whenever you like. We hope you join us soon!

Watercolor Class

Tuesday, 5PM - 6PM Learn how to watercolor! We'll be teaching all the different watercolor techniques.

Date Night Painting

Thursday, 6PM - 7PMSip on your favorite drink while painting with your friend or date! Each week we have a different guided painting to create, using different styles and techniques. Not only will you create something beautiful to hang on your wall, but you'll also have a fun, relaxing evening.

Kids' Craft Class

Wednesday, 5PM - 5:45PMIs your child a budding artist? Let them unleash their creativity at our craft classes, where they'll be creating a new project every week!
